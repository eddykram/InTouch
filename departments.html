Our Departments

These are our Departments

CEO

Our Chief Executive Officer, the highest ranking person in a company or other institution, ultimately responsible for making managerial  decisions.

COO

Our Chief Operating Officer is vital because the effectiveness of COOs is critical to the fortunes of many companies and could be to many more.

CFO

Our Chief Financial Officer oversees a company's finances. A CFO analyzes financial data, reports financial performance, prepare budgets, and monitors costs. Assessing investments.

CMO

Our Marketing officer is the corporate executive responsible for an organizations marketing activities. The CMO's primary responsibility is to generate revenue by increasing sales through: Brand management, Marketing communications, and Market research.  

Auditing

In our Auditing department, our workers monitor each of of the other departments in the firm for errors, misstatements and fraud. In addition, Auditing manages the operation and security of technology systems such as file servers and website, as to ensure that the digital aspect of the firm stays fully functioning and is protected against any digital threats that may occur. Furthermore, auditing engages in internal audit controls where they verify accounting for accuracy and improperties.

Human Recources

In Human Resources department, our workers strive to provide a secure and comfortable work environment for all our employees. They are mainly tasked with maintaining the records of employees in the firm, including monitoring employee's attendance as well as employee production, which is done by the implementation of employee self-evacuation sheets. Human Resources is also in charge of resolving any conflicts between employees should the situation occur. And finally, the Human Resources department works in conjunction with the Accounting department to make sure that every employee is paid on a timely basis.

Research and Development

Our Research and Development department is, our dedicated team endeavors to make sure that the technological aspect of our firm is unrivalled amongst our competitors. They are in charge of the development and production of the firm's consumer products, which includes both the hardware and software aspects of our products. Also, Research and Development manages the official InTouch website, which includes the design and features of the website, as to ensure that the website is the most reliable and efficient as it can possibly be.

Accounting

In our Accounting department, our experienced team, led by our Chief Financial Officer, is tasked with managing the company's finances and transactions to make sure action is fully accounted for. Specifically, they manage financial transactions with other firms, along with employee payrolls, and record them in a coherent and organized spreadsheet. In addition, accounting is responsible for making payments to the VE government, which includes both taxes and one time payment.

Marketing

In our Marketing department, our team studies and analyzes the market to suggest certain strategies to help market our product better. Examples include analyzing the target market, where the Marketing Team has to determine the ideal demographic for our products and the composition of promotional advertisements, such as product brochures, to be distributed at trade fairs and other networking events.

Sales

Our sales department is our skilled team works hand-in-hand with the Marketing department to implement and execute the strategies devised by Marketing. In addition, Sales is responsible for making contact with other firms in order to negotiate business deals, either through a phone call or by email. Finally, Sales works with the social media department to record promotional videos to be published on the InTouch social media platform.

Social Media

Our Social Media department is our knowledgeable team exploits the popularity of social media platforms like Twitter and Instagram to market our products and develop a strong brand recognition for our firm. In particular Social Media posts news on both our firms and products, and is able to respond to any comments, in order to develop a strong relationship through social media and to increase our overall popularity.
